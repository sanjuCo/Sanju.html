RECIPE: 
Chicken Fried Rice

Ingredients:  

  • 2 cups of cooked white rice(preferably cooled)
  • 2 chicken breasts, diced
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables(peas, carrots, corn)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil(optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Green onions, chopped, for garnish

Instructions:  

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or work ober medium-heigh heat
  2. Add the diced chicken and cook until it's no longer pink, then remove it from the skillet and set it aside
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil and minced garlic. Saute for a minute until fragrant
  4. Push the garlic to the side and pour the beaten eggs into the skillet. Scramble them until they are fully cooked
  5. Add the cold, cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps. Stir-fry the rice for a few minutes.
  6. Add the mixed vegetables ad the cooked chicken to the rice, continuing to stir-fry until everything is heated thorough.
  7. Pour soy sauce(and sesame oil, if using) over the rice and stir to combine. Deasin with salt and pepper ton taste.
  8. Cook for a few more minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the rice to get a bit crispy.
  9. Garnish with he chopped green onions and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade chicken fried rice!

Feel free to adjust the ingredients and seasonings to your taste.
